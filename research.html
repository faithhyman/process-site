Interview questions: Ludlow- Owner


1. What exactly are the missions that Lulas is raising money for?
Started Lulas with intent of original idea to connect people with missions. They wanted a positive atmosphere where people could donate, and they could also promote other non-profits. Lulas wants to raise money for people around the world as well as our local community. They focus and raise money on bigger topics such as sex trafficking, but also support these things on a smaller scale within the community. They also started a water well in Tanzania.
2. What is thread and compass and how does it relate to Lulas?
Thread and compass is the non-profit they created to own Lulas. What would be profit goes into thread and compass. Lulas and thread and compass are the same.
3. Why the monkey for the logo, and does it represent anything?
Inherited the monkey, might go away soon. The reason for it is because of a mission that they worked with in Peru that had a pet monkey named Renaldo.
4. What about your site is working, what isn’t?
They don’t have a site but have a place for landing page. Have an ordering page. Nothing specifically stated.
5. Tell me a little about the owners/ is there a way I could get a picture and bio for each of them for the site.
He stated he could get me this.
6. Do you guys have a specific slogan that you would want to use?
Not right now.
7. What direction do you see Lulas going towards in the next few years?
They just expanded to the second location. Hopefully coming out of pandemic, hoping to get back into missions very soon. Been on standstill recently with the pandemic. They want to continue their heart for ministry and have that be at the front of the brand.
8. Anything else that you would specifically want to see in the site or how I could help you with this?
Nothing specifically!

User: Julianna Gray, High School Senior


1. What is a fond memory at Lulas?
Having conversations with people and catching up with them over a cup of coffee. Seeing my friends and family play and sing has also been really cool.
2. How do you feel that Lulas has impacted the community?
Lulas has shaped a community of people who love coffee and people. It provides a light for young and college aged students and a place for them to escape to when they need peace and quiet.
3. What are three words you would use to describe Lulas?
Peaceful, fun, light-hearted.
4. What is something that you will remember about Lulas for your whole life?
I will remember the open mic nights. Seeing everyone cheering others on and having fun while doing it was so cool to watch. And seeing the community get involved with something like that was also so fun to watch.
5. What could change about Lulas?
Honestly, nothing.
