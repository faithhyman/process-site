Design Challenge

Questions that will aid in the route I am going to take to redesign Lula's website.

What is the problem you are trying to solve?
Show through the website in a more impactful way the mission and goals of Lula's Coffee Company as well as make the site easier to use.
1)The Design Question
How can this website make people want to buy coffee from Lulas to support missions?
2)The Ultimate Impact
To bring more awareness and money to the mission work that Lulas is working with.
3)Possible Solutions
-Make mission statement more apparent
-High quality images
-More functional and aesthetic website design
-More personal and genuine feel to the site
-Different set of hierachy
4)Context and Constraints
Lulas is targeted towards students and workers. Use this idea when coming up with marketing and design strategies. Make is easily navagational, but also aesthetic.
5)The Design Question (restated)
How can the way I design and project the mission of this site create a want in people to buy their coffee from Lulas?
